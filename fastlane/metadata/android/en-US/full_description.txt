Simple app to manage your flashcards.

app structure
filing boxes -> decks -> flashcards

Filing boxes can be exported as and imported from a backup.

The decks of a filing box contain the flashcards.

Flashcard values: front, back, notes (optional) and progress (integer).

You may activate flashcard formatting in the app settings.

You may use four color variants for filing boxes and decks.

Please backup your content before installing updates.
