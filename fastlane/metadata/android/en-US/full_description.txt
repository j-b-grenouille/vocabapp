Very simple app to manage your flashcards.

app structure
filing boxes -> decks -> flashcards

Filing boxes can be exported as and imported from a backup.

The decks of a filing box contain the flashcards.

Flashcard values: front, back, notes (optional) and progress (integer)

NOTE
This app is a very basic app for all who can't handle more complex apps without getting confused. You won't find many features, e.g. there is no query mode.

DISCLAIMER
This app is provided on an as is basis. Backup your content before installing updates. Development might be discontinued any time.
